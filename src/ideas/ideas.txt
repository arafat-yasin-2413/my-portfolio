text ideas
----------


1. Minimal and Professional
    Heading: Poppins (bold)
    Body: Open Sans or Roboto

2. Elegant & Classy
    Heading: Playfair Display
    Body: Lato
    ✅ Good for portfolios that want a more refined, artistic feel.

3. Modern & Sleek
    Heading: Montserrat
    Body: Inter or Nunito
    ✅ Great for web developers, startups, UI/UX portfolios.

4. Creative & Friendly
    Heading: Raleway (extra bold)
    Body: Quicksand
    ✅ Adds personality while keeping it readable.

5. Tech & Futuristic
    Heading: Orbitron or Exo 2
    Body: Source Sans Pro
    ✅ Perfect for software engineers or game dev portfolios.
